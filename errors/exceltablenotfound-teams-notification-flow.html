ExcelTableNotFound in Teams Notification Flow

The Excel connector cannot find the named table in the workbook. This article focuses on the teams notification flow scenario.

Medium PP-1963 Power Platform Excel connector HTTP 404

Applies To

Power Automate cloud flow posting to Microsoft Teams

Typical Scenario

The automation posts adaptive cards or channel messages after a business event.

What This Error Means

The Excel connector cannot find the named table in the workbook. This article focuses on the teams notification flow scenario.

Common Causes

Workbook has no formatted table, table renamed, file copied without expected structure, or connector cached stale metadata. In this scenario, confirm the team, channel, connector permissions, message payload, and DLP policy for the environment.

How To Resolve It

Open the workbook, confirm the table name, refresh the connector action, and select the correct table. For teams notification flow, also verify: Confirm the team, channel, connector permissions, message payload, and DLP policy for the environment.

How To Prevent It

Use stable table names and avoid manual workbook structure changes. For teams notification flow, document the expected permissions, connector settings, and test data used to validate the fix.
