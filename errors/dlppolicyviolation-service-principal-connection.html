DlpPolicyViolation in Service Principal Connection

A Data Loss Prevention policy blocks the connector or connector combination used by the app or flow. This article focuses on the service principal connection scenario.

High PP-0840 Power Platform DLP policy HTTP 403

Applies To

Power Platform connection using app registration or service principal authentication

Typical Scenario

A flow, app, or integration authenticates with an application identity instead of a personal user account.

What This Error Means

A Data Loss Prevention policy blocks the connector or connector combination used by the app or flow. This article focuses on the service principal connection scenario.

Common Causes

Connector assigned to blocked group, business/nonbusiness group conflict, or environment added to a restrictive policy. In this scenario, check app registration permissions, consent, secret expiry, certificate validity, security roles, and connection ownership.

How To Resolve It

Review the environment DLP policies, identify the blocked connector, and request an approved policy change if justified. For service principal connection, also verify: Check app registration permissions, consent, secret expiry, certificate validity, security roles, and connection ownership.

How To Prevent It

Check DLP impact before adding connectors to production solutions. For service principal connection, document the expected permissions, connector settings, and test data used to validate the fix.
